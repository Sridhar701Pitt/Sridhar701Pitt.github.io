About

Credit: Max Pixel

Hey this is Sridhar. I'm a graduate Electrical Engineer currently pursuing my Master's degree in Robotics at Georgia Tech.

I think robotics is such an amazing field where you get to build and play with a toy and make an impact to a real life application. Just being able to learn to write simple instructions to command this cool plaything brings me joy and satisfaction. Though the learning curve sometimes becomes steep and seems unattainable, the drive to succeed is strong and keeps me moving to achieve a greater reward in the end.

Anyways, my current interest is in developing control algorithms for aerial robots making them agile, efficient, and cool. Though there are many childhood factors driving my craze for robots, this TED talk by Raffaello D'Andrea is why I wanted to work specifically in aerial robotics. The way aerial robots such as quadcopters move in the air with such precision and agility (like in drone racing, and videography) seems so super human and awe inspiring. My other interest is in multi-agent systems which one day would allow me to command a swarm of drones becoming my new super power.
